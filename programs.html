Academic Programs

Explore our comprehensive range of engineering programs designed to prepare you for success in today's dynamic technological landscape.

Undergraduate Programs

All our undergraduate programs are 4-year Bachelor of Science degrees with ABET accreditation, featuring hands-on learning, research opportunities, and industry internships.

Computer Engineering

BS Degree

Merge hardware and software expertise to design and develop computing systems, from microprocessors to supercomputers and embedded systems.

Key Focus Areas:
  • Digital Systems Design
  • Computer Architecture
  • Embedded Systems
  • Network Security
  • Artificial Intelligence
Career Paths:

Hardware Engineer, Systems Architect, Embedded Systems Developer, Network Engineer, AI Specialist

Admission Requirements

Mechanical Engineering

BS Degree

Design, analyze, and manufacture mechanical systems, from small components to large machinery, vehicles, and energy systems.

Key Focus Areas:
  • Thermodynamics & Fluid Mechanics
  • Materials Science
  • Robotics & Automation
  • CAD/CAM Systems
  • Renewable Energy Systems
Career Paths:

Mechanical Designer, Automotive Engineer, HVAC Specialist, Robotics Engineer, Manufacturing Engineer

Admission Requirements

Civil Engineering

BS Degree

Plan, design, and oversee construction and maintenance of infrastructure projects including buildings, bridges, and transportation systems.

Key Focus Areas:
  • Structural Engineering
  • Geotechnical Engineering
  • Transportation Systems
  • Environmental Engineering
  • Construction Management
Career Paths:

Structural Engineer, Transportation Planner, Construction Manager, Geotechnical Engineer, Urban Planner

Admission Requirements

Electrical Engineering

BS Degree

Design and develop electrical systems, power generation and distribution, electronics, and control systems for various applications.

Key Focus Areas:
  • Power Systems
  • Electronics & Circuit Design
  • Control Systems
  • Signal Processing
  • Renewable Energy
Career Paths:

Power Systems Engineer, Electronics Designer, Control Systems Engineer, Telecommunications Specialist, Renewable Energy Engineer

Graduate Programs

Advance your engineering career with our master's and doctoral programs, featuring cutting-edge research and specialized expertise.

Master of Science

2-year research-focused programs with thesis and non-thesis options available.

  • M.S. in Computer Engineering
  • M.S. in Mechanical Engineering
  • M.S. in Civil Engineering
  • M.S. in Electrical Engineering

Master of Engineering

Professional practice-oriented programs designed for working engineers.

  • M.Eng. in Engineering Management
  • M.Eng. in Sustainable Engineering
  • M.Eng. in Systems Engineering

Doctoral Programs

Research-intensive Ph.D. programs for those pursuing academic or advanced research careers.

  • Ph.D. in Engineering Sciences
  • Ph.D. in Applied Engineering
