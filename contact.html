Visit Our Campus

Campus Location

Our campus is located in the heart of Tech City, easily accessible by public transportation and with ample parking available for visitors.

Directions

From Downtown: Take Innovation Expressway north to Exit 15. Follow signs to Engineering College.

Public Transit: Take the Blue Line to Innovation Station. Campus is a 5-minute walk from the station.

Parking

Visitor parking is available in Lot A near the Main Administration Building. Daily parking passes can be obtained at the Security Office.

Common Questions

How can I schedule a campus tour?
You can schedule a campus tour by calling our Admissions Office at (555) 123-4567 or by using the online tour scheduling system on our website. Tours are available Monday through Friday at 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM, and select Saturdays at 11:00 AM.
What should I bring to my campus visit?
We recommend bringing comfortable walking shoes, weather-appropriate clothing, and any questions you have for our admissions team. You may also want to bring a notebook to take notes during your visit.
Can I meet with faculty during my visit?
Yes, we can arrange meetings with faculty members in your area of interest with advance notice. Please indicate this preference when scheduling your visit, and we'll do our best to coordinate a meeting based on faculty availability.
Is overnight accommodation available for prospective students?
Yes, we offer overnight stays with current students for high school seniors who have applied to Engineering College. These visits are typically available from September through April and must be scheduled at least two weeks in advance.
