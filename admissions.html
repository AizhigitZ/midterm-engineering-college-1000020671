Admissions

Begin your journey to becoming an engineering leader. Learn about our admission process, requirements, and important deadlines.

Application Process

1

Check Requirements

Review the admission requirements for your desired program to ensure you meet all criteria.

2

Prepare Documents

Gather all required documents including transcripts, test scores, and recommendation letters.

3

Submit Application

Complete and submit your online application through our secure portal.

4

Interview

Selected candidates will be invited for an interview with the admissions committee.

5

Receive Decision

Admission decisions are typically communicated within 4-6 weeks of application completion.

Admission Requirements

Undergraduate Requirements

Completed application form
Official high school transcripts
Minimum GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale
SAT score of 1200 or ACT score of 25
Two letters of recommendation
Personal statement (500-750 words)
Mathematics through Pre-Calculus
Laboratory Science (Physics and Chemistry)

Graduate Requirements

Completed application form
Bachelor's degree in engineering or related field
Minimum GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale
GRE scores (optional for 2023-2024)
Three letters of recommendation
Statement of purpose
Current resume/CV
Proof of English proficiency (for international students)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application fee?
The application fee is $75 for domestic students and $100 for international students. Fee waivers are available for qualified students with demonstrated financial need.
Do you accept transfer students?
Yes, we welcome transfer students from accredited institutions. Transfer students must have a minimum GPA of 2.8 and completed prerequisite courses in mathematics and science.
Is on-campus housing guaranteed?
On-campus housing is guaranteed for all first-year students who submit their housing application by May 1st. Upperclass students have priority based on credit hours completed.
Can I visit the campus before applying?
Absolutely! We offer campus tours Monday through Friday and special weekend open houses throughout the year. You can schedule a visit through our website or contact the admissions office.
